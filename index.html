How to Play

  1. Each player selects their move in secret
  2. Click a piece, then click destination square
  3. Confirm your move
  4. When both players are ready, submit moves
  5. Both moves execute simultaneously!

Conflict Resolution

  • Mutual Capture: Both pieces are removed
  • Square Collision: Lower-value piece wins!
  • Piece Values: King(100), Queen(9), Rook(5), Bishop/Knight(3), Pawn(1)
  • Same Value: Both pieces removed
